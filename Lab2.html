Bonchon is a globally recognized Korean fried chicken restaurant chain that has expanded far beyond its roots in Busan, South Korea. Since its founding in 2002, Bonchon has become known for its crispy double-fried chicken glazed in signature soy garlic or spicy sauces, as well as its fusion of Korean and Western flavors.

Today, Bonchon operates in hundreds of locations across Asia, the United States, the Middle East, and Europe, making it one of the most internationally successful Korean food brands.
